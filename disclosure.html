Affiliate Disclosure

Last updated: June 2026

In short: when you click a product link on Findskart and make a purchase, we may earn a commission from the retailer. It costs you nothing extra, and it never decides what we recommend.

What this means

Findskart participates in affiliate marketing programs. This means that some of the links to products on our site are "affiliate links." If you click one of these links and make a purchase, we may receive a small commission from the retailer or brand at no additional cost to you.

Why we use affiliate links

Running Findskart — testing products, maintaining the site, and paying our small team — costs money. Affiliate commissions are how we keep the site free to use without charging for access or relying on intrusive advertising.

This does not affect our recommendations

  • Products are selected based on our own testing and research, not on commission rates.
  • A brand paying for a placement does not guarantee a spot on Findskart — we don't accept payment for editorial inclusion.
  • If a product we've recommended stops being good, we remove or update the listing regardless of any affiliate relationship.

Price accuracy

Prices shown on Findskart are checked regularly but may change at the retailer's discretion. Always confirm the final price at checkout on the retailer's site before completing your purchase.

Questions

If you have any questions about our affiliate relationships or how we select products, reach out at hello@findskart.com or visit our contact page.
