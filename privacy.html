Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

We collect the minimum information needed to run Findskart well — things like what pages you visit and, if you sign up, your email address. We don't sell your personal data.

Information we collect

When you visit Findskart, we may automatically collect certain information through cookies and similar technologies, including your browser type, device type, pages visited, and how you arrived at our site. If you subscribe to our newsletter or contact us through a form, we collect the information you provide, such as your name and email address.

How we use this information

  • To operate and improve the site, including understanding which pages and products are most useful to visitors.
  • To send newsletter emails if you've subscribed, which you can unsubscribe from at any time.
  • To respond to messages sent through our contact form.
  • To track affiliate link clicks, which helps our retail partners attribute purchases back to Findskart.

Cookies

Findskart uses cookies for basic site functionality and analytics. Affiliate links may also set cookies on behalf of our retail partners so that purchases can be correctly attributed. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, though some site features may not work as intended.

Third parties

We work with affiliate networks and retail partners who may collect information when you click through to their sites. Their use of your information is governed by their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

Your choices

You can unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time using the link in any email we send. You can also contact us to request information about data we hold about you or to ask that it be deleted.

Contact us

Questions about this policy can be sent to hello@findskart.com.
